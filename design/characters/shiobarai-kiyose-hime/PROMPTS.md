## 共通固定

22歳前後の健康的でしなやかな水の聖女。青緑から海硝子へ変わる長髪、淡い水色の瞳、白い聖女衣、水青の羽衣、真珠と波の刺繍、肩と腰の水晶板、三つ桃の水紋飾り。女性向け和風異世界ラノベ、精密なアニメ顔、幻想的な油彩仕上げ。文字、枠、透かし、別人物を入れない。

## 10・神札

```text
Vertical oracle card. She stands where a sacred spring meets the sea, holding the translucent Water-Boundary Fan. Clear water divides into a stream carrying away dark murk and a stream returning clean light. Full figure, rain-cleared morning, compassionate practical strength.
```

## 11・魂札「引キ受ケタ濁リ」

```text
Vertical oracle card. She kneels in a shallow moonlit pool, holding several borrowed vessels while dark ink-like water weighs down only the outer sleeves. Her own clear water glows beneath her. Exhausted and tender, non-horror, no body corruption.
```

## 12・行札「他人ノケツマデ拭クナ」

```text
Vertical oracle card. She rises and sweeps the Water-Boundary Fan; borrowed dark water separates from her sleeves and returns across a luminous channel to empty vessels on the far bank. Sunrise after rain, relieved practical smile. No people and no literal butt or cleaning tools.
```
