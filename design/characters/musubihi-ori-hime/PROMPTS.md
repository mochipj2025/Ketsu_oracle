## 共通固定

26歳前後の堂々とした契約公爵令嬢。緋色から金へ変わる長い波髪と高いまとめ髪、蜂蜜色の瞳、緋色の公爵令嬢ドレスと打掛の融合、銅金の刺繍、組紐、指輪、封蝋、三つ桃の契約印。女性向け和風異世界ラノベ、精密なアニメ顔、幻想的な油彩仕上げ。文字、枠、透かし、別人物を入れない。

## 13・神札

```text
Vertical oracle card. Before a divine loom in an open treaty hall, she guides one crimson and one gold strand into an equal braided bridge. Paired rings and wax seals glow around the loom. Full figure, warm sunset, commanding and welcoming; threads never bind her body.
```

## 14・魂札「独リ織リ」

```text
Vertical oracle card. Alone at midnight in an empty hall, she tries to repair an enormous torn tapestry. Too many obligation threads run to rings on her fingers and pull in different directions. Dignified but tired, unused scissors beside the loom, no injury or bondage.
```

## 15・行札「ケツヲ組メ」

```text
Vertical oracle card. She cuts one overburdening thread, opens both hands, and equal crimson-gold cords braid into a bridge toward an unseen opposite shore. A matching ring glows in answer. Sunrise, relief and confidence. No other person and no literal butt imagery.
```
